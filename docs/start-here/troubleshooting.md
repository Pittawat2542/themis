# Troubleshooting

Common categories:

- `run_id` changed unexpectedly: [Identity vs provenance](../explanation/identity-vs-provenance.md)
- Resume or report cannot find the run: [Choose your storage backend](choose-your-storage-backend.md) and [Resume and inspect runs](../how-to/resume-and-inspect-runs.md)
- Provider adapter setup is failing: [Install extras and configure providers](../how-to/install-extras-and-configure-providers.md)
- You are unsure whether logic belongs in a reducer, parser, or metric: [Reducer vs parser vs metric](../explanation/reducer-parser-metric-boundaries.md)
- Workflow-backed metrics behave differently than pure metrics: [Use workflow-backed metrics](../how-to/use-workflow-backed-metrics.md) and [Metric families and subjects](../explanation/metric-families-and-subjects.md)
